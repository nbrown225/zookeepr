PORTS
- A website is like a college campus
Address = Host
building/classroom = Port 

Port 80 used for sites with http://
Port 443 used for https://
Ports 1024 and under are special/require permissions

Ports range from 1024 - 49151
Port 3001 is common practice


REQ.PARAMS VS REQ.query
req.query is multifaceted, often combining multiple parameters, whereas req.param is specific to a single property, often intended to retrieve a single record.
